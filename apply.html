Keep an eye out...

We are not recruiting at this time. We will resume recruitment at the beginning of the Fall 2024 semester, and it will coincide with the Fall 2024 Project Team recruitment timeline. Please check back in early August.

How can I prepare?

In the meantime, there are a few things you could do to prepare for our next recruitment cycle.

  • Reach Out!

    Feel free to reach out to us at cuautodrone@gmail.com. We check this email regularly, so do reach out with any questions about the team, or indicate your general interest!

  • Prepare Your Resume

    A resume, while optional, is a very important part of our application process. Do your best to prepare a good resume that shows why you would be a good fit.

  • Interview Prep

    If we decide to move forward with your application, you can expect an interview containing a mix of technical and non-technical questions. For technical questions, we look for how you go about solving them, not necessarily the right answer.

  • Follow Our Social Media

    Our social media pages linked below will contain information on recruitment - when it starts, where to apply, etc. Follow us on social media to be in the know!
